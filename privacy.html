Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 22, 2026

TrueRack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using TrueRack, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

TrueRack is a community-powered clothing sizing web app built and operated by an independent developer based in Toronto, Canada.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address — collected when you create an account.
  • Username — chosen during profile creation, displayed on community submissions.
  • Body measurements — chest, waist, shoulders, hips, inseam, and body length.
  • Size chart submissions — brand name, product name, size data, and measurement notes.
  • Reviews and votes — body length feedback and true-to-size votes on brand cards.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser localStorage — measurements, voting state, and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Account sync data — if signed in, measurements and preferences are synced to our database.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect payment information, device identifiers, IP addresses, or browsing behavior beyond what is described above. We do not use tracking pixels or third-party advertising cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized size recommendations based on your measurements.
  • To display your username on community brand submissions you create.
  • To aggregate community votes and reviews shown on brand cards.
  • To sync your measurements and preferences across devices when signed in.
  • To send transactional emails related to your account (verification, password reset).
  • To send product updates if you opt in — you can opt out at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (supabase.com) — database and authentication. Your account data is stored on Supabase servers. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Gemini API (ai.google.dev) — parses size charts you submit. Text you paste is sent to Gemini for processing. See ai.google.dev/terms.
  • Netlify (netlify.com) — app hosting. May log standard server access data. See netlify.com/privacy.

We do not sell your data to any third party.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active.
  • Measurements — retained in Supabase while your account exists; localStorage until you clear it.
  • Community submissions — retained indefinitely unless you request removal.
  • Anonymous votes — stored in localStorage only, not linked to your account.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data. Canadian users have rights under PIPEDA. US users in applicable states may have additional rights under state privacy laws.

To exercise your rights, use the in-app data request form (coming soon) or contact us directly at rashid.mekail@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

TrueRack is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us.

8. Data Security

We use Supabase's built-in Row Level Security (RLS) policies and industry-standard authentication encryption. No security system is perfect, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated Effective Date. Continued use of TrueRack after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
