Cookie & Storage Notice
Effective Date: June 22, 2026

TrueRack does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. This notice explains how we use your browser's built-in storage to make the app work.

What We Use Instead of Cookies

TrueRack uses localStorage — a browser feature that stores data locally on your device. Unlike cookies, localStorage data is never automatically sent to our servers. It stays on your device until you clear it.

What We Store in Your Browser

Measurements & Preferences

  • Your body measurements — so the app auto-fills on return visits.
  • Your unit preference (cm or inches).
  • Your active category and any custom categories you've created.
  • Dark mode preference and other display settings.

Onboarding State

  • Whether you have completed the guided app tour — so it doesn't repeat on every visit.

Anonymous Voting

  • A record of which brands you have voted on (true-to-size and body length) — so you can't vote twice on the same brand. This data is stored only on your device and is not linked to your account.

When You Sign In

If you create an account and sign in, your measurements and preferences are also synced to our Supabase database. A session token is stored in localStorage to keep you signed in — this is used only for authentication, not tracking.

How to Clear Your Storage

You can clear TrueRack's localStorage data at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome / Edge: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data → Site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data
  • Safari: Settings → Advanced → Website Data → Remove All Website Data

Clearing storage will remove your saved measurements from your device. If you have an account, your data will still be available when you sign back in.

No Advertising Trackers

TrueRack does not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other advertising or behavioral tracking tools. If we add analytics in the future, this notice will be updated.
